Skill Swap

Allows students to exchange skills directly with each other, fostering collaboration and mutual growth.
For example, a graphic designer could exchange services with a programmer to create a portfolio website.

Web Development

The process of designing, building, testing, and deploying a web application from start to finish. It involves working with both the front-end and back-end of a website or application.

App Development

The process of creating, testing, and releasing software applications to meet business needs and improve customer experiences.
